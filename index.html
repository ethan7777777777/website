FAQ

Answers before you book the Free Scan.

Does HIPAA compliance cover CCPA?

No. HIPAA protects PHI; CCPA regulates personal information, including marketing data, loyalty apps, and website tracking. Both are required for California clinics.

What do you need from our team?

One point of contact, access to intake forms, vendor list, and your website CMS. Everything else happens asynchronously over our secure workspace.

Can you work with franchised med spas?

Yes. We coordinate with corporate legal, map shared systems, and deliver location-level packets that satisfy franchise audits.
