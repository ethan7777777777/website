Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 13, 2026

Overview

ComplianceCurrent provides CCPA risk audits and compliance support for med spas. This Privacy Policy explains the categories of personal information we collect, the purposes for collection, and the rights available to California residents.

Categories of Personal Information We Collect

  • Identifiers: name, business name, email address, IP address.
  • Commercial information: selected plan and transaction records.
  • Internet activity: pages visited, site interactions, and device/browser metadata.
  • Professional information: number of locations and business website URL.

Sources of Information

  • Directly from you when you submit our form or contact us.
  • Automatically from website usage and basic analytics/cookie technologies.
  • From service providers who support payment, hosting, and infrastructure operations.

Business and Commercial Purposes

  • Provide requested scans, reports, and customer support.
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions.
  • Maintain security, detect abuse, and debug technical issues.
  • Improve product quality and service performance.
  • Meet legal, compliance, and contractual obligations.

Disclosure to Service Providers

We may disclose personal information to service providers that perform services on our behalf, including hosting, payment processing, and analytics support. These providers are contractually restricted to processing data for authorized business purposes.

Sale/Sharing and Your Choices

We provide a Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information page to submit opt-out requests. We also honor Global Privacy Control signals where required by law.

Retention

We retain personal information only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, including service delivery, recordkeeping, security, and legal compliance.

California Privacy Rights

  • Right to know/access categories and specific pieces of personal information.
  • Right to delete personal information, subject to legal exceptions.
  • Right to correct inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to opt out of sale/sharing of personal information.
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights.

To submit a request, visit Privacy Request, email complianceconcurrent@gmail.com, or call (916) 661-1844.

Notice Regarding Sensitive Information

We do not request detailed patient treatment records through our intake form. If sensitive personal information is processed, we apply heightened safeguards and limit use to legally permitted business purposes.
